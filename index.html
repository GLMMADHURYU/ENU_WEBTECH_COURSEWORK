Classic Porridge Meal

The recipe requires the following ingredients: - 1 cup of rolled oats (or corn grits) - 2 tablespoons of Chia seeds - 5 heaping tablespoons of 100% ground cacao - 2 heaping tablespoons of chocolate peanut butter swirl - 5 cups of water - 1 cup of coconut milk (or Greek yogurt) - Sweetener, such as date syrup or coconut sugar - 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract or vanilla pods/beans - Peel of 1 orange - 7 pitted and chopped dates per portion - Fresh fruit slices (such as raspberries, blackberries, sliced banana, grated apple, pear, and orange segments) for each portion - Optional: A pinch or two of cinnamon To prepare the recipe, start by bringing the water to a simmer in a saucepan. Then, add the ground cacao and chocolate peanut butter swirl, stirring until they are fully dissolved. Turn off the heat and add the rolled oats and Chia seeds. Stir constantly to avoid sticking and cook for 3-5 minutes until the porridge is ready. To sweeten, add the date syrup. Serve the porridge in bowls and top each portion with a drizzle of coconut milk or a spoonful of Greek yogurt. Additionally, add chopped dates, fresh fruit slices, and a pinch of cinnamon, if desired.

Banana Energy Bowl

Ingredients: 1 cup of dark chocolate chips 2 ripe bananas 1 tablespoon of coconut oil 1/4 cup of chopped nuts (optional) 1/4 cup of shredded coconut (optional) Instructions: Melt the dark chocolate chips in a double boiler or in the microwave. If using a microwave, heat the chocolate in 30-second increments, stirring after each interval until fully melted. Once melted, add the coconut oil and stir until fully combined. Pour the melted chocolate mixture into small silicone bowls or muffin tins, making sure to coat the bottom and sides evenly. Place the bowls or tins in the fridge and chill until set, about 30 minutes. While the chocolate bowls are chilling, slice the bananas into thin rounds. Once the chocolate is set, remove the bowls or tins from the fridge and carefully remove the chocolate bowls from the molds. Fill the chocolate bowls with sliced bananas and top with chopped nuts and shredded coconut, if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Creamy IceCream Sticks

Ingredients: 1 cup raw cashews, soaked in water for at least 4 hours 1 can full-fat coconut milk 1/2 cup maple syrup or other sweetener of choice 1 teaspoon vanilla extract Pinch of salt 1/2 cup chopped cashews for topping Instructions: Drain and rinse the soaked cashews and add them to a high-speed blender. Add the can of coconut milk, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt to the blender. Blend the mixture on high speed until smooth and creamy. Pour the mixture into a container with a lid and freeze for at least 6 hours or overnight. Once the ice cream is frozen, remove it from the freezer and let it sit at room temperature for 10-15 minutes to soften. While the ice cream is softening, chop the 1/2 cup of cashews for the topping. Scoop the ice cream into serving bowls and sprinkle the chopped cashews on top. Serve immediately and enjoy! Optional: You can also add chocolate chips, caramel sauce, or any other toppings you like to the ice cream.
